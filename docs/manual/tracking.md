# Track dense cells from maps

```{figure} ../_static/diagrams/diagram-distro-tracking.svg
:alt: The ITASC ingredient board with Ultrack, Ultrack correction, EpiCure editing, and Core lit; the Cellpose maps it consumes and every other piece dashed.
:figclass: only-light

**itasc-tracking** ships the Ultrack nucleus tracker and both its correction tools, on the
shared Core. It consumes the foreground and contour maps you supply. Solid chips are shipped;
dashed are the rest of the family.
```
